Some of my photography.

    I have always enjoyed photography and fine automobiles. These classic Ferraris were displayed at the opening of the Behring Museum in Blackhawk Plaza, Danville, CA.

    This photo was taken in the Summer of 1962, in the New York City showroom of Luigi Chinetti. They were the first importer of Ferrari to the U.S., and the North American Racing Team was run from this location, which fielded Ferraris in major auto races since the early 1950's.

    This one-of-a-kind Cadillac was built in 1954 by world-famous designer Pinin Farina. As seen here, it was on display at the Pebble Beach Concours d'Elegance.

    Could this be the first 'Selfie'? This photo was taken at a book signing event in Los Angeles in 1979. The intended subject: Ray Bradbury, the noted Science-Fiction/Fantasy author. Unfortunately, with this type of photo, the photographer tends to dominate the scene...

    These flowers were on a dwarf peach in our backyard.

    This is the flower of the succulent Epiphyllium.
