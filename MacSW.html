Computer Wizards’ Mac Shareware

    (usable on MacOS 7.x - 9.x)

    • AboutFinder - a StartupItems utility to always display the "About This Macintosh" window.

    • .Restart - A Desk Accessory to reboot your Mac; similar to .Shutdown.

    • Stack Editor - A HyperCard developers' tool for old (1.x) versions of Apple's wonderful toolkit.

    • Stack Compare - A HyperCard developers' tool for Apple's wonderful toolkit.

    • Quick Pick - A HyperCard stack to select lottery numbers. Includes an XFCN to generate random numbers.
